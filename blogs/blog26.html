There was a girl named Aarohi...

Yeah, you people might say - “Again, Aarohi’s story?”

So yesss, my lovely readers... it’s again Aarohi.

The Time Aarohi was doubting herself-

She stood at the edge, the world behind her echoing with doubt.

Then, with one step forward, she rewrote her fate...

So, my readers - don’t let doubts surround you.

People might think you’re wrong, or even crazy...

But when you truly understand yourself,

What others think of you just doesn’t matter....
