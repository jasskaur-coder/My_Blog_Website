Day 27 – “How Writing Daily Changed the Way I Think”

← Back

July 13, 2025

If I clearly remember, I started writing in my School Days - a time that remains one of the most precious phases of my life to this day. I was struggling with exams, board pressure, and all the chaos that comes with it. Of course, those things put immense pressure on the mind.

There was a lot going on in my head… how to study, how I would manage everything. And then, one day, I wrote a small quote. If I recall correctly, it was: "Don’t trust anyone, not even your shadow… because it stays with you only as long as there is light."

I don’t know how that thought came to me, but I wrote it down and posted it on Instagram. Day by day, I started writing more quotes. One of my favourites is:
"Hard work along with smart work, in the right direction with focus, is the key to success."

So every time I got stuck, I started writing - and surprise, surprise… it saved me. Every single time......

Sometimes, I think my writing skills aren’t that great. Maybe they’re just average. But then the second thought always hits me: I’m not craving perfection in writing.
It’s my passion, the one thing that drives me - like a flowing river.
